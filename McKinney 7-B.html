Is Coconut Cult Worth It?

Coconut Cult is a super-live probiotic coconut yogurt that is supposed to help with digestion. They use 16 species to ensure that customers get the full benefits.

In order for the probiotics to work, they need to be alive. Probiotics come alive through prebiotics, like food. Coconut Cult can help with bloating, inflammation, digestion, immunity, and overall health.

It is recommended to eat the coconut yogurt first thing in the morning with breakfast or as an after dinner snack. Since the yogurt is so concentrated, it is recommended to eat only a tablespoon a day.
